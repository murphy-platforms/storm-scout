About Storm Scout

Storm Scout is a site-focused dashboard that consolidates active advisories and operational signals by location so we can quickly identify which testing centers may be impacted and support go/no-go decisions. It's built to reduce manual checking and keep attention on the sites and markets that matter most during weather events.
